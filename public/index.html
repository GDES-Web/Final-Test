Glow Fest 2023

Glow Fest is a modern music festival that aims to create an immersive experience for young adults. The festival is inspired by neon lights and vibrant colors, with an emphasis on electronic dance music. The target audience is adults aged 18-30 who enjoy music, dancing, and socializing.
